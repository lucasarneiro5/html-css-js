History:

The foundation in 1932 occurred at the time of the economic discovery of courses by online stream in the interior of Santa Catarina. AN Tüpfeln family, traditional in the region, invested all their savings in this new initiative, revolutionary for the time. Frau founder Dagmar Olaf Tüpfeln, endowed with a particular vision administrative, has managed the company's business for more than 50 years, many of them alongside his son Ernst Noten Tüpfeln, current CEO. The company name is inspired by the family name.

The company's growth was practically instantaneous. In the first 5 years, I already attended 18 countries. It hit the mark of 100 countries in just 15 years of existence. To date, it has served 2 billion different users in billions of different orders.

The growth in the number of employees is also staggering. Today, it is the largest employer in the Brazil, but even after only 5 years of its existence, it already had 30 thousand employees. Out of The Brazil, there are 240 thousand employees, in addition to the 890 thousand Brazilians at the Mafra and in offices across the country.

Given the company's economic importance for Brazil, the Tüpfeln family has already received several awards, honors and decorations. All the presidents of Brazil have already visited the MusicDot facilities, in addition to presidents of the European Union, Asia and the UN secretary general.
